Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Digital Products

Since Aptitude Labs provides non-tangible, irrevocable goods (Online Mock Tests), we generally do not issue refunds once the product is purchased and accessed.

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds ONLY under the following specific circumstances:

  • If a technical error prevents you from accessing the test series entirely, and our support team fails to resolve it within 72 hours.
  • If a double payment has been made accidentally for the same course.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original source of payment.

4. Contact Us

For any billing issues, please email us immediately at aptitudelabshost@gmail.com.
